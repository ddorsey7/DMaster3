The Interview

  • High Concept: Your man with a grudge, roaming other cars for revenge.
  • Player Experience Goals: The experience I want the player to feel is fun and stress at the same time.
  • Genre: A casual game, like candy crush, with little strategy and more timing.
  • Game play: You play as the road rager in the gray car, roaming the red cars. The road rager must avoid the pursuit of the cop car as well as hit as many cars as he can. For every car you hit you gain 10 points. If the cop hits you, you lose 20 points. To win you must get a 1000 points within one minute.
  • Prototype goal: The game mechanic this is prototype evaluating the best movement and balance for difficulty.
  • Features: The unique aspects of my game that set it apart is it road rage without the GTA carnage. It's humorous, simplistic, and fun.
  • Setting/Premise: Your a cat trapped in a box, avoiding killer bouncing balls for a whole minute.
  • Story: Your late for work again because of this traffic. F@#% this traffic. F#@% this system! F%#@ them all!
  • Target Audience: People have nothing else to do. Mostly likely children from ages 3-9.
  • Play time: One minute
  • Strategies: Player strategies that will be effective at playing this game would be not knocking any of balls and move slowly.
  • Prototype Assets: Assets use where a cat drawing, ball animation, and a desposit photo of a box.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Games that most closely resembles my game are "Bounce and Knock", "accelerate to pointer", and "Group vs. Self."
